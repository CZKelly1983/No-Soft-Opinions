Institutional Blind Spots

What Film Culture Sees
That Audiences Don't

Films where the Filmmaker's Film score (F2) significantly exceeds the Popular Verdict (F1). The gap measures a specific kind of cultural disconnect — films the industry prizes, that filmmakers study, that critics rank among the greatest ever made, but that most audiences have never seen or thought about. These are not obscure failures. They are the films that shaped cinema invisibly.
